Save the World

It has been 37 months since the outbreak, the nation is all but lost, and everyone is just trying to survive.

Ann has discovered what she believes is the cure to fight the disease that plagues the world.

It is up to Ann, Johnathan, and Micheal to travel south to the University of Colorado, where the anti-virus can be mass produced before it is too late.
