About Us

No 'Micro Transactions'

Here are Antagonising Entertainment we believe that there should only be a 'One-Time-Fee' for our content.

This means that any additional content provided to any of our games will be included for free. All that is required of you is that first time purchase of our games.

No 'Pre-Orders'

We feel that pre-ordering games for bonuses is wrong.

You should not feel pressured to buy any of our games until they are released.
