About Me

A boy that grew up into a man loving to play video games with the dreams of making his own some day

General Info

I am the 3rd eldest of 7 children in a family of 9. I grew up in love with sports, and in love with video games. I am happily married to the love of my life, we currently don't have any kds, but we do have a dog.

Professional Info

I went to college so that I could learn skills to develop video games. I started by getting my Associate of Arts Degree, but I didn't find the skill set that I was looking for. I continued going to school where I pursued getting my Bachelor's of Science degree in Computer Science. I'm currently a Software Engineer working as a contractor for a staffing agency company.

Gaming Background

When I was younger I played platformer games like Super Mario, and Donkey Kong when my family would go see my Grandparents. I got heavily involved with playing video games when I was in middle school. I first played shooter games like Halo, and Socom, then later got into single player games like Tales of Symphonia, Paper Mario, and Fire Emblem. Once I finished High School I got into playing World of Warcraft and after a short while I raided non-casually with friends for about 8 years.

Goals

My future goals are to make Let's Play videos and began developing my own video games. I went to school in order to pursue my dream of being a Video Game Designer, and I plan on starting off with making smaller games at first and then depending on how successful they are build a small company where my team and I could make larger games.
